Youtube Seminar

This seminar is based on the fact that on Youtube (and on other streaming services as well) there are talks given by outstanding mathematicans and physicists. The setup of the seminar would be that we watch a talk together and discuss during and after the talk about its content. AS a sidenote the original idea for this seminar arose in a discussion with Erfan Rezaei.
