Research profile

I am broadly interested in several aspects of geometry and mathematical physics. Some of my current projects are:

  1. Non-existence theorems for harmonic maps.
  2. Understanding the structure of the moduli space of the extended Bogomolny equation.
  3. Universal Higgs bundles and properties of canonical sections.
  4. Deformations of the character variety of the once punctured torus.
